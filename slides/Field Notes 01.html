We do not launch an agent for a client until it can answer five questions.

Newtuple  ·  On agent readiness
The five questions 1 of 5
01 Can it explain exactly what it will and won't do?
02 Can it handle inputs it was never trained for?
03 Does it know when to stop and escalate to a human?
04 Can every action be logged, audited and reversed?
05 Has it been tested against its own failure modes?
